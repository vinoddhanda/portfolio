## Overview

India has millions of small agri-input shops — fertilizer and pesticide dealers serving farmers in every district. These shopkeepers manage inventory worth lakhs of rupees, run credit for dozens of farmers, and track purchases from multiple vendors — all in paper khatas.

**AgroKhata** replaces that paper khata with a digital system built around how shopkeepers actually work: in Hindi, on the shop floor, hands busy, often mid-conversation with a customer.

> **Built the full product — design, frontend, AI integrations, database — solo in 3 days using AI-native tools.** Figma for design, Claude Code for development, Claude API for voice parsing and bill OCR. What would have taken a team of 4 several weeks, shipped in a weekend.

## The Problem

A fertilizer shop owner in Haryana or Punjab typically speaks Hindi, not English. They get physical bills from vendors, run credit for farmers who pay after harvest, and stock 20–50 products — all tracked manually.

**The paper khata fails at scale.** Finding a customer's balance means flipping through pages. Knowing stock means counting bags. When credit goes untracked, money is lost. The challenge was making digital faster and easier than paper for someone who has never used a business app.

### फोटो लो, AI पढ़ेगा। Entry होगी।

![AI Bill Scan — point camera, AI extracts items and fills entry automatically](/images/projects/astrokhata/profile-bill-scan-ai.png)

When a vendor delivers stock with a physical paper bill, the shopkeeper used to re-enter every item manually. With **AI Bill Scan**, they just point their phone camera at the bill — the app extracts items, quantities, rates, and vendor name in seconds. One tap to confirm and save as Stock In, Sale, or Payment.

**This eliminates the biggest daily data-entry burden.** For a shop receiving 2–3 deliveries a day, that's real time saved every single day.

> Scan bill → AI reads & parses → Confirm & save

---

### बोलो, AI समझेगा। Entry होगी।

![AI Voice Entry — speak in Hindi or local dialect, AI parses to structured transaction](/images/projects/astrokhata/profile-voice-ai.png)

Many shopkeepers are comfortable speaking but uncomfortable typing on a phone. **Voice Entry** lets them say a transaction out loud in natural Hindi — and the app parses it into a structured entry.

Say *"मनीष को दोनों में गोल्ड 550 रुपए ना गाड़ी"* — colloquial, abbreviated, mid-transaction — and it correctly parses to: Sale · Nominee Gold · ₹550 · Cash. No menus, no typing. Speak one sentence, confirm, done.

> Tap & speak → AI parses → Confirm & save

## What Else We Built

- **Ledger (हिसाब)** — real-time dashboard: बिक्री, खरीद, मिला, बकाया — all live, in Hindi
- **Stock** — every product with buy/sell price, quantity, and automatic low-stock alerts
- **Customer accounts** — per-customer credit tracking with due amounts highlighted in red
- **Quick actions** — one `+` tap from anywhere opens New Sale, Stock In, Payment, Expense, Scan Bill, Voice Entry
